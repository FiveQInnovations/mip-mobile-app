TestFlight Installation Instructions for FFCI App

Hello!

Thank you for agreeing to test the FFCI mobile app! We've invited you as an external tester, and you should have received an email invitation from Apple TestFlight. Follow these simple steps to get started:

QUICK START: Install TestFlight from the App Store, then accept the email invitation you received and tap "Start Testing" to open TestFlight. Once in TestFlight, find "FFCI" under Available Apps and tap Install to get the beta build.

STEP 1: Install TestFlight

1. Open the App Store on your iPhone or iPad
2. Search for "TestFlight" (it's Apple's official beta testing app)
3. Tap "Get" or the download button to install TestFlight
4. Wait for the installation to complete

Note: TestFlight requires iOS 14 or later. If you're using an older device, you may need to update your iOS version first.

STEP 2: Accept the Invitation

1. Check your email inbox for an invitation from Apple TestFlight (it may be in your spam/junk folder)
2. Open the email and tap the "Start Testing" button or link
3. This will open the TestFlight app automatically, or you can manually open TestFlight and the invitation will appear

STEP 3: Install the FFCI App

1. Once you've accepted the invitation, open the TestFlight app on your device
2. You should see "FFCI" listed under "Available Apps"
3. Tap on "FFCI"
4. Tap the "Install" button next to the available build
5. Wait for the installation to complete (this may take a few minutes depending on your internet connection)

STEP 4: Launch and Test

1. Once installed, you can launch the FFCI app from your home screen (it will have an orange dot badge indicating it's a TestFlight build)
2. Start testing the app and let us know if you encounter any issues or have feedback!

IMPORTANT NOTES

- Beta builds expire: Each beta build is available for up to 90 days. When a build expires, you'll need to install a new build if one is available.
- Automatic updates: You can enable automatic updates in TestFlight settings so you'll always have the latest beta version.
- Feedback: If you encounter any bugs or issues, please let us know! You can also use TestFlight's built-in feedback feature by shaking your device while using the app.
- Production version: When the app is released to the App Store, you'll need to download the official version separately.

TROUBLESHOOTING

- Can't find the invitation email? Check your spam/junk folder, or let us know and we can resend it.
- TestFlight app won't open? Make sure your device is running iOS 14 or later.
- Build not showing up? Make sure you've accepted the invitation first, and that you're logged into TestFlight with the same Apple ID that received the invitation.

ADDITIONAL RESOURCES

For more information about TestFlight, visit Apple's official site: https://testflight.apple.com/

If you run into any issues or have questions, please don't hesitate to reach out!

Thank you for helping us test the FFCI app!
